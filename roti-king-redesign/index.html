Roti King Restaurant

Roti King Restaurant is specialised at delivering delicious authentic Malaysian dishes in London. Hidden but with increasing popularity, Roti King is situated in a basement in Kings cross in short walking distance from Euston metro/train station.
‍
The restaurant is small and simple, the inside is cosy with open kitchen to have the luxury to see Roti Canai made on the fly! The staff are very friendly and always happy to help.

Menu

Roti canai is a type of Indian-finfluenced flatbread found in Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia. It is often sold in Mamok stalls (in Malaysia, it is known as voti prata) in Southern Malaysia and Singapore and is similar to the Indian Kerata parafta.
